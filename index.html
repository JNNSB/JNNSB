Wondering what is JNNSB?

JNNSB is the IEEE student branch of Jwaharalal Nehru New College of
Engineering. It is one of the most active student branches of Karnataka. We
organize and host a wide range of technical workshops and events. We have our
odd sem fest Anveshana,Mysterio and even sem fest Plasama,Techzone are few
our signature events.

And, why JNNSB?

JNNSB conducts various kinds of workshops, seminars, webinars,
technical talks, competitions, hackathons, and events througout the
semester to help its members for the all round development with the
advancing technology.

A Part of IEEE Managalore Section

IEEE Mangalore Sub-Section was formed on 19th April 2014 with the aim to conduct various student and professional activities
like invited talks, student internships, workshops and the conferences. The Engineering colleges from Mangalore, Udupi,
Karwar, Shivamogga, Chikmagalur district comes under this Sub-Section. At present, it has more than 1000 IEEE Members in
total including Professional and Student Members.
